Patricia A. Suriana is a software engineer at Google Research, working on Halide, an open-source DSL designed specifically for computational photography. She received an MEng in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2016, where she was advised by Saman Amarasinghe, and she received a BSc in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science also from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2014. Her research interests include programming language, distributed system, high-performance computing, and path planning.
